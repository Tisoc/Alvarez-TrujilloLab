Sara Pedroza

Jackelin Mendoza received her Bachelor degree in 2014 in Chemical Pharmaceutical Biology from the Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León (UANL) where she worked as volunteer in the Laboratorio de Biología Molecular assisting in research for diagnosis of Closteroviridae infection in orange trees. Her professional experience includes clinical pharmacology and physicochemical analysis of samples of environmental interest. Currently she is working in isothermal-amplification based devices for molecular diagnosis of viral infections as part of her MSc studies in the Tecnológico de Monterrey.
