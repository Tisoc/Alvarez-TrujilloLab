David Choy

David Choy Buentello is a PhD student at Technologico de Monterrey’s DBT Bioengineering program. David completed his bachelor’s Degree at Sacred Heart University, Majoring in Biology and minoring in Chemistry and Mathematics. His research project involved measuring axonal growth of different transfected neuronal cultures. His Master’s was completed at the University of Hartford. Thesis work was completed at the University of Connecticut, where David created neuro- anatomical maps of the inferior colliculus. David also completed a year of graduate studies at the Pennsylvania State University using MRI technologies to study neurodegenerative diseases, specifically Parkinson’s. David has joined our lab hoping to use previous skills and integrating them to 3D printing and matrix culturing.
