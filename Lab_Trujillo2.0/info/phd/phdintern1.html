Alan Márquez

Alan Márquez received his B.Sc degree as Chemical Pharmacobiologist from Universidad Autónoma de San Luis Potosí (2008), and his M.Sc. degree in Molecular Biology and Genetic Engineering from Universidad Autónoma de Nuevo León (2011). Since august, 2012 he is conducting his PhD in Biotechnology at ITESM. Under the direction of Dr. Mario Álvarez, his doctoral work involves the obtaining of relevant proteins such as influenza antigens and antibody derived peptides to functionalize nanoparticles to provide vaccine candidates and detection systems for some antigens. Alan is a proactive researcher with experience in the field of recombinant proteins production and purification processes, skilled in design and construction of expression systems by molecular biology and genetic engineering techniques with expertise in recovering of products by chromatographic processes.
